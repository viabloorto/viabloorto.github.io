Toronto's Next Great Neighbourhood


Downtown Toronto’s next great neighbourhood is coming to Bloor Street. Spread across four city blocks of master-planned magnificence, and STUNNING VIEWS overlooking Rosedale Valley! Via Bloor is a tale of two buildings set to become a neighbourhood of people, public spaces and culturally rich experiences. Envisioned as a new retail promenade, Tridel will be extending Bloor Street’s famed shopping to create Via Bloor.
